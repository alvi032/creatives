HOW IT WORKS

Many creators wonder, how does your service work? What makes it different to stand-alone editors? This page is designed to answer that question.

  • Client briefly describe us the requirements and share data/raw footage with us through google drive or wetransfer

  • A producer and dedicated editor will begin work on the video. Producer being experienced in the field support the editor in decision throughout the post production process to save client’s valuable time being used up answering questions

  • 1st draft is reviewed by the producer along with a senior editor. Discussing each part of the video and 15-40 adjustments are made in this step

  • Producer reviews the adjustments and sends over the video to client at the agreed time/day

  • The client reviews the video, leaving feedback (if any). Editor immediately revise the video

  • Producer review client’s comments and make sure all the adjustments are accurate and final render is sent to the client to review

  • If still any small adjustments are required which is quite rare, then these will be made in line with the Client’s uploading schedule
