HOW IT WORKS

Many creators wonder, how does your service work? What makes it different to stand-alone editors? This page is designed to answer that question.

STEP 1

The creator uploads the footage to Google Drive, along with any text notes (or voice notes in video format), for us to reference to. Also, answers a short list of questions from us, to ensure mutual understanding of requirements for each video

STEP 2

The producer (also known as manager) and editor will begin work on the video, the producer being available to support the editor in decisions throughout the video, to avoid the creator’s time being used up answering questions

STEP 3

The editor uploads the video to an internal Frameio folder. The producer then reviews the video, along with another editor familiar with the content, leaving feedback and discussing different parts of the video with the editor. Typically 20-70 adjustments are made at this stage

STEP 4

The editor revises the video, re-uploads the video, the producer does a final review and then sends over to the client at the agreed time of day/deadline

STEP 5

The client reviews the video, leaving their feedback (if any is needed). Once the feedback is left by the client, the editor will (immediately) revise the video based on the client notes

STEP 6

The editor and producer discuss the notes left by the client, ensure they agree on how the notes should be implemented, and then do a final render to present to the client

STEP 7

The video is then complete. If any further small adjustments are required (rare), then these will be made in line with the creator's uploading schedule.
